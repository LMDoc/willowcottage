This single storey, detached cottage is located in the village of Penistone.


French doors from the sitting and dining area, take you out to a pretty patio area where you can make the most of a sunny afternoon with a refreshing drink.


For an extra touch, a swimming pool is available for use when not hired out to swimming clubs and the owner can confirm specified times; sure to make a lovely addition to your holiday.


Why not explore the village of Penistone with its open countryside, woodlands and weekly market. There is plenty to see in this spectacular location.

Where are we?

Less than 5 miles from J37 of the M1, Willow Cottage is located in the grounds of Willowlodge at Hoylandswaine, on the outskirts of Penistone.

Directly off the A629 (Halifax Road) approx. 200m from Hoylandswaine Roundabout when heading in the direction of Huddersfield, the first driveway on th right-hand side, signed Willowlodge.
